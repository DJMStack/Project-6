About

Our menu features a variety of dishes, from classic fish and chips to more unique offerings like our famous seafood pasta. We source our seafood from local fishermen, ensuring that our ingredients are of the highest quality and sustainably caught. At our restaurant, we believe that the dining experience is just as important as the food itself. That's why we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all of our guests. Whether you're dining with friends or family, our attentive and friendly staff will make sure you have an enjoyable time. In addition to our delicious food and welcoming atmosphere, we also offer a full bar with an extensive selection of beers, wines, and cocktails. Our knowledgeable bartenders are always happy to help you find the perfect drink to complement your meal.
